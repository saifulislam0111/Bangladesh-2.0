Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh 2.0 Innovation symbolizes the nation's ambitious drive toward technological advancement, sustainable development, and a knowledge-based economy. It focuses on fostering startups, digital transformation, and inclusive growth to position Bangladesh as a global leader in innovation.

Sustainability

Bangladesh 2.0 Sustainability emphasizes a transition toward green energy, climate resilience, and eco-friendly practices to address environmental challenges. It aims to integrate sustainable development goals into every sector, ensuring economic growth while preserving natural resources for future generations.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh 2.0 Infrastructure envisions modernizing transport, energy, and urban systems to create a more connected and efficient nation. It prioritizes smart cities, renewable energy integration, and resilient infrastructure to support rapid economic growth and improve quality of life.

Empowerment

Bangladesh 2.0 Empowerment focuses on uplifting communities through education, skill development, and equal opportunities for all. It aims to foster an inclusive society where youth, women, and marginalized groups actively contribute to and benefit from the nation's progress.
